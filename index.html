About

The Global Initiative on Artificial Intelligence for Health (GI-AI4H) was launched by the three United Nations (UN) Specialized Agencies ITU, WHO and WIPO in July 2023 at the AI for Good Global Summit in Geneva (press release).

  • Open workshops and meetings
  • Across the globe

GI-AI4H convenes meetings and workshops open to all across the globe.

  • Open standards
  • Open code

GI-AI4H develops open standards and code.

Framework

The GI-AI4H continues work of its predecessor FG-AI4H (2018-2023) on an Artificial Intelligence for Health Framework with horizonal standards on subjects like regulation, ethics, clinical evaluation, benchmarking, etc. and vertical standards on medical topics (such as cardiology, dermatology, dentistry).
