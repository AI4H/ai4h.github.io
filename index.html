AI for Health Framework

We continue the work of the GI predecessor (ITU/WHO Focus Group (2018-2023)) on an "Artificial Intelligence for Health Framework": a collection of horizonal standards on topics like regulation, ethics, clinical evaluation, benchmarking, etc. as well as vertical standards on medical areas, suchs as cardiology, dermatology, dentistry, etc.
